About

I am PhD student in computer science advised by Andrew McCallum at the Information Extraction and Synthesis Laboratory (IESL) at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am broadly interested in natural language processing and machine learning. My current interests involve extraction of structured semantic representations from text under settings of limited labelled data. I am also advised by Brendan O'Connor and work closely with Jeff Flanigan on this project.
